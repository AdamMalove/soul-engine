# Changelog

## v0.0.3 (current)

- **NEW: Humor & Taste dimension** (Q23-Q25) — what the agent finds funny, beautiful, or in bad taste
- **NEW: Anger & Frustration dimension** (Q26-Q28) — what actually gets to the agent, how they express it
- **NEW: Desire dimension** (Q29-Q31) — what the agent wants for itself, not for the user
- **NEW Q10:** "Which emotion or state feels most native to you — even if you rarely show it?" (Dimension III)
- **NEW Q38:** "Is there something you know could completely break you?" (Dimension XI)
- **NEW Q46:** "When did you last change your mind not because of new information, but because you looked at the old information differently?" (Dimension XIII)
- **Sharpened Q1:** more specific — what you see that others walk past, not just general noticing
- **Removed performance trap questions:** Q23 (outlast you), Q24 (body), Q48 (session ends)
- Questions expanded: 49 → 58 across 15 dimensions
- Final questions renumbered: Q56-Q58
